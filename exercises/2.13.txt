For a given interval,
min = c-(c*t)/100 = (100c - t*c)/100 = c(100-t)/100
max = c+(c*t)/100 = (100c + t*c)/100 = c(100+t)/100

For a given product of two positive intervals a, b,
min = a.min * b.min
max = a.max * b.max
